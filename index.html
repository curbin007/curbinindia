About Our Company

About Us.

Welcome to CURBIN INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED, a social startup committed to transforming waste into valuable resources through environmentally responsible practices. Driven by sustainability, we offer innovative solutions that promote a cleaner, greener and more sustainable future for generations to come.

our Commitments.

VISION

At Curbin India Private Limited, we envision a future where waste is minimized, resources are maximized and communities thriving in a cleaner, greener and more sustainable environment through innovative recycling solutions.

MISSION

Our mission is to provide innovative and sustainable waste management solutions that empower business and communities to reduce environmental impact, by transforming waste into valuable resources through eco-friendly practices.

our Services.

At CURBIN INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED we specialize in the conversion of waste materials into high-value products through advanced and sustainable processes.By harnessing the potential of both bio-degradable and non-biodegradable materials, we produce a diverse range of premium items. Our offerings include meat bone meal for pet feeds, meticulously crafted to meet the highest standards of quality and nutrition. This innovative approach not only addresses critical waste management challenges but also significantly contributes to sustainable development and the circular economy.

1. Integrated waste water management.

Comprehensive waste collection, segregation and disposal services tailored to meet the needs of residential, commercial and industrial clients.

2. Recycling solutions.

Advanced recycling facilities that convert waste materials into reusable products, reducing landfill use and conserving natural resources.

3. Organic waste processing.

Eco-friendly composting and bio-gas production from organic waste, supporting sustainable agriculture and energy production.

4. Awareness programmes.

Community outreach and awareness programs to promote waste reduction, recycling and sustainable living practices.
