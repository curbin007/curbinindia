Solid Waste Management

Plastic Collection Initiative

Our goal is to reduce plastic pollution in households and rivers, protect aquatic ecosystems, and promote a cleaner, healthier environment. Through this initiative, we supply specialized bins designed for the efficient collection and disposal of plastic waste.

Our Focus Areas:

  • Pollution Reduction: Removing plastic waste from rivers and beaches to prevent environmental contamination.
  • Community Engagement: Encouraging active community participation in keeping our waterways clean.
  • Sustainable Solutions: Providing durable, effective bins for long-term use in cleanup efforts.
